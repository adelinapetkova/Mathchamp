Are you a fan of mathematics? Do you like competitions?

If your answer to these questions is Yes, then you are in the right place. Mathchamp is a site for anyone who likes competition to be a part of their daily routine. Whether you are at the beginning of your education or on the final line, everybody is welcome here to solve some interesting math problems in order to prove himself as the Math Champion.

We want the competition to be as fair as possible and this is the reason why you will compete with students who are at the same grade as you. Teachers will provide for you all kinds of math problems with different difficulty. According to its difficulty each task will give you a specific number of points, which will help you gain a higher position in your grade’s ranking.

You have solved all the provided tasks, but you are thirsty for more? Go and ask your math teacher to join our group of mathematicians and help us have a bigger diversity of questions for our students.

Take a look at our example task:

Volume

Tom wants to know how much food his container can hold. The container is 20 centimetres tall, 10 centimetres long and 10 centimetres wide. What is the container’s volume?
